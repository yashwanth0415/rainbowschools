About Us

A little school with a big heart

Sri Rainbow School was built on one simple belief: children learn best when they feel safe, seen, and delighted by what's in front of them. Our classrooms are bright, our teachers are warm, and our days are full of wonder.

Our Mission

To nurture confident, kind and curious children through play-based, joyful learning experiences that respect each child's own pace.

Our Vision

A school where every colour of every child is celebrated — and where learning feels like discovery, not duty.

Safe & Caring

CCTV-monitored campus, trained caregivers, hygienic meals and a nurse on call — because peace of mind matters to parents too.

Small Class Sizes

Low student-teacher ratios mean every child gets seen, heard, and encouraged — every single day.
