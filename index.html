Our Story

More than a school — a second home.

Founded with a simple belief — that every child deserves to learn in an environment filled with colour, curiosity, and care — Sri Rainbow School has been shaping confident, kind, and creative young minds. Our classrooms buzz with questions, our playgrounds ring with laughter, and our corridors echo with the rhythm of growing up.

Recognized by the Government of Telangana, we offer a nurturing space where children from Play Group to Class 5 discover the joy of learning — every single day.
