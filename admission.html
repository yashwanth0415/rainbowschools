FAQ

Frequently asked questions

We've answered the most common questions parents have. If you don't find what you're looking for, reach out to us anytime.

What is the age criteria for admission?
For Play Group, children should be at least 1.5 years old. For Nursery, the minimum age is 2.5 years, LKG is 3.5 years, UKG is 4.5 years, and Class 1 is 5.5 years as of the academic year starting April.
What is the student-teacher ratio?
We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of 12:1 for Play Group and Nursery, and 18:1 for LKG and above. This ensures every child receives individual attention and care.
Do you provide transportation?
Yes, we provide safe and reliable school transport within a 5-7 km radius of the campus. All vehicles are GPS-tracked and have trained attendants on board.
What is the admission process?
The process is simple: enquire, visit the campus, submit documents, and confirm admission. We guide you through every step and are always available to help.
Is there an entrance test for admission?
For Play Group and Nursery, we have a simple interaction-based assessment to understand your child's readiness. For LKG and above, we have a basic readiness assessment — no high-pressure tests.
What are the school timings?
Our school operates Monday to Saturday, 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM. Timings may vary slightly for different age groups — we'll share the detailed schedule during your campus visit.
