Our Story

How a small dream grew into a colourful community

Sri Rainbow School began in 2006 with a handful of children, a single bright classroom, and a big belief: that every child deserves a childhood filled with curiosity, kindness, and colour.

From one classroom to a whole rainbow

What started as a tiny preschool in Hyderabad quickly became a trusted name in early education. Parents loved the warm atmosphere, the caring teachers, and the way their children blossomed — not just academically, but emotionally and socially.

Today, Sri Rainbow School is a vibrant community of over 1,200 students, 60 dedicated teachers, and a curriculum that spans from Play Group to Class 5. But we've never lost the heart of what made us special: a school where every child feels seen, celebrated, and safe.

We remain proudly recognised by the Government of Telangana and guided by the belief that learning should be joyful, inclusive, and full of wonder.

Mission & Vision

What drives us, every single day

Our mission and vision are the colours that guide everything we do — from how we design our classrooms to how we welcome each family.

Our Mission

To nurture confident, kind, and curious children through play-based, joyful learning experiences that respect each child's own pace and personality.

  • Foster a love for learning from the very first day
  • Build emotional and social skills alongside academics
  • Create a safe, inclusive space for every child

Our Vision

A school where every colour of every child is celebrated — and where learning feels like discovery, not duty. A place where children grow into confident, compassionate individuals ready to embrace the world.

  • A school that feels like a second home
  • Where curiosity is the curriculum
  • Building a foundation for lifelong learning

Our Values

The colours that guide us

At Sri Rainbow School, values aren't just words on a wall — they live in our classrooms, our playgrounds, and in every interaction.

Love & Care

Every child is wrapped in warmth and kindness, building trust and emotional security from day one.

Curiosity & Wonder

We encourage questions, exploration, and the joy of discovery in every subject and activity.

Inclusion & Respect

Every child is unique, and we celebrate those differences while nurturing mutual respect and friendship.

Excellence & Effort

We believe in doing our best — not just in academics, but in character, creativity, and community.

Safety & Trust

A secure environment where children and parents alike feel confident and at ease.

Joy & Play

We believe childhood should be filled with laughter, movement, and the freedom to imagine.

Facilities

A campus made for little hands and big ideas

Every corner of our school is designed with children in mind — bright, safe, and full of possibilities.

Bright, Spacious Classrooms

Colourful, well-ventilated rooms with child-sized furniture and learning corners for every subject.

Safe Playground

A large, shaded outdoor space with age-appropriate swings, slides, and climbing structures.

Library & Reading Nook

A cosy library filled with picture books, storybooks, and early readers to spark a love for reading.

Art & Craft Studio

A dedicated space for painting, clay work, crafts and creative expression.

Music & Movement Room

Instruments, rhythm, and dance — a space where children explore sound and motion.

CCTV & Security

24/7 monitored campus with trained staff and secure entry protocols for complete peace of mind.
