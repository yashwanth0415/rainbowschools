Our Programs

Learning that fits every age and stage

We believe children learn best when they're engaged, happy, and challenged just enough. Our three developmental stages are carefully crafted to meet each child where they are.

1.5 – 3 yrs

A gentle introduction to school life through sensory play, rhythm, and routines that build trust and independence.

  • Sensory & motor skill play
  • Music, rhymes & movement
  • Toilet training support
  • Social & emotional bonding
  • Circle time & storytelling
  • Art & messy play
3 – 5 yrs

LKG & UKG

Phonics, numeracy, art and storytelling woven into a structured, playful school day that builds foundational skills.

  • Phonics & early literacy
  • Numbers & pattern play

  • Social skills & collaboration
  • Introduction to writing

A strong academic core — language, math and science — balanced with sport, art and values education for holistic growth.

How We Teach

A pedagogy rooted in wonder

Our teaching approach blends proven educational philosophies with the joy of discovery. Here's what makes our classrooms special.

Play-Based Learning

We believe play is the highest form of research. Our classrooms are designed for exploration, experimentation, and joyful discovery.

Child-Centred Approach

Every child is unique. We tailor our methods to individual learning styles, paces, and interests — no two children are the same.

Hands-On & Experiential

Children learn by doing. From science experiments to art projects, we bring concepts to life through tangible, sensory experiences.

Social-Emotional Learning

We nurture empathy, resilience, and emotional intelligence alongside academics — because feelings matter as much as facts.

Integrated Curriculum

Subjects are woven together through themes and projects, helping children see connections and think holistically.

Collaborative Learning

Children learn from each other. Group work, peer mentoring, and circle time build communication and teamwork skills.

Beyond the Classroom

Where talents blossom and friendships bloom

We believe a well-rounded education goes beyond textbooks. Our co-curricular activities nurture creativity, confidence, and a lifelong love for learning.

Music & Dance

From rhythm to melody, children explore sound, movement, and expression through joyful sessions.

Art & Craft

Painting, clay work, collage — every child discovers their inner artist in our creative studio.

Sports & Athletics

Team games, yoga, and fitness activities that build strength, coordination, and sportsmanship.

Nature & Gardening

Children connect with the natural world through gardening, nature walks, and environmental projects.

Storytelling & Drama

Imagination comes alive through stories, role-play, and puppet shows that build language and confidence.

Public Speaking

Show & Tell, poetry recitation, and class presentations help children find their voice and express themselves.

Yoga & Mindfulness

Simple breathing exercises, stretches, and mindfulness practices that promote calm and focus.

Computer & Tech

Age-appropriate introduction to computers, digital literacy, and creative tech projects.

Assessment

Watching them grow, every step of the way

We assess progress with care and kindness — focusing on growth, not grades. Our approach helps us understand each child's journey and support them where they need it most.

Assessment that celebrates progress

We use a combination of ongoing observation, portfolios, and simple benchmarks to track each child's development across academic, social, and emotional domains.

  • Continuous, day-to-day observation
  • Individual learning portfolios
  • Age-appropriate worksheets & activities
  • Regular parent-teacher conversations
  • No high-stakes exams — just joyful growth
